About

NaffKo 54 are a 4 piece band bringing you the ultimate pop punk party.

Playing the best pop punk, punk rock and emo anthems from the 90s and 00s, including songs from Blink-182, Green Day, The Offspring, Sum 41, My Chemical Romance, Fall Out Boy, New Found Gory, Good Charlotte and many more.

We're based in Devon, gig regularly all over the South West, as well as travelling further afield "up north".

Contact

The best way to get in touch is via one of our social media channels, with the quickest response usually Facebook Messanger.

Or drop Paul an email on contact@naffko54.com.
